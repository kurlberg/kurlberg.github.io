KTH/SU Mathematics Colloquium

22 February 2006

Avgust Tsikh, Krasnoyarsk

Amoebas and multidimensional difference equations

ABSTRACT We use the language of amoebas of algebraic hypersurfaces to get a multidimensional analog of the fact that all roots of a univariate polynomial have distinct moduli. This translation allows us to obtain a multidimensional analog of the classical result on the asymptotics of solutions to a difference equation due to H.~Poincare. Restrictions in applicability of the latter result are closely related to possible generalizations of Pick's formula relating the area and the number of integer points in a polygon.
