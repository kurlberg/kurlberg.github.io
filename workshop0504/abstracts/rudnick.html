Zeév Rudnick, Tel Aviv University

Moments of L-functions

The moments of central values of families of L-functions have recently attracted much attention and, with the work of Keating and Snaith, there are now precise conjectures for their limiting values, originally arrived via Random Matrix Theory. We develop a simple method to establish lower bounds of the conjectured order of magnitude for several such families of L-functions.

As an application we can show that the fluctuations of matrix elements for the Laplacian on the modular domain are not Gaussian, contrary to what is expected in the generic case for chaotic systems. (Joint work with K. Soundararajan)
