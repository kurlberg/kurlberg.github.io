Fredrik Strömberg, Uppsala University

Computational aspects of Maass cusp forms with nontrivial multiplier systems

I will discuss some new methods that allow us to compute Maass waveforms in a general setting. In particular we can now treat groups with any number of cusps and Maass waveforms of any real weight and transforming according to any multiplier system. For those that don't know what a weight or a multiplier system is: the physical interpretation of a Maass waveform with weight and multiplier system is that of a quantum mechanical particle moving on a surface where we have introduced a magnetic field. (The weight corresponds to the field strength and the multiplier system to a consistent setup of the magnetic fluxes through the cusps).
